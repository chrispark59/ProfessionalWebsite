The Crosswind Project

The Crosswind Project

A world building project that imagines a future where astroid mining is a mainstream source of critical materials. Through the user of Blender I built each of these models using hard surface modeling techniques I learned from YouTube and BlenderBros. I used various addons and learned about the Blender addon workflow.

Parallelism

Human cognition and machine embodiment operate as one system across long distances. Asteroid mining is made possible through robotic units deployed in space, controlled directly by human operators on Earth. By linking their minds to these machines, people can efficiently harvest material without the dangers of space.

Extraction

To extract materials operators require demolition tools to efficiently harvest material. These devices are engineered to break down material with precision, balancing brute force and control in zero-gravity environments. High-power chipping tools handle delicate, localized operations where accuracy is critical, while this integrated laser cutter enables the clean removal of larger mineral sections.

Transportation

Through the use of a drilling and collection astroid mobility unit, operators are able to both extract denser material and move material from point A to point B.
