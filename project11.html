A gamafied echolocation experience

Volume Zero

A virtual reality game exploring an abandoned temple through the use of echolocation. Built using the Quest and Unity, the game makes use of a shader graph to create light waves that illuminate the users evnrionment. When a player speaks they envrionment lights up depending on the pitch of their audio. The player must navigate and escape the environment while also conserving how much they speak. While speaking lights up the environment, it also alerts a blind monster to the location of the player.
