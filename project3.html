Tesla Model S

Tesla Model S

This project was an exploration of industrial automobile design and cinematic product rendering.

Skills

  • Blender

Process

I took a modular approach, dividing the car into 4 parts and working on them sequentially. I used Blender to model and render the car. I used a 3-point light scheme to create a more cinematic render.
