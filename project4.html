Red Bull

Red Bull Product

Red bull is a product that I drink a lot on late nights when I am building renders. This project was an attempt at hyper-realism for a real product. Lighting, texturing, the timeline, and graph editor were the most important tools I used to make this. For texturing I used Blender geometry nodes to create the metal and ice textures. For lighting I used a 3 point light scheme to create an equal combination of blue and red. The graph editor and timeline were used to efficiently develop animations.
